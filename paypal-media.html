What data to bring

Required data

How to connect via Snowflake
1
Accept the PayPal Media collaboration invite
PayPal Media sends a Snowflake Collaboration request to your Snowflake account. Accept it in your Snowflake console under Data → Collaborations → Incoming.
2
Load your data into a Snowflake table
Stage the required data (see above) into a table in your account. Hash any PII fields — emails and phone numbers — using SHA-256 before loading.
3
Grant access to the collaboration
Grant the PayPal Media collaboration role read access to your table. This is a scoped, read-only grant — PayPal cannot write to or export your data.
4
PayPal Media confirms the connection
Our team validates the data schema and confirms the collaboration is ready to run. Typically within 1 business day of access being granted.
PII handling: All customer identifiers must be hashed (SHA-256) before loading. Never share raw emails, phone numbers, or postal addresses. The collaboration schema enforces this — unhashed fields will be rejected.
