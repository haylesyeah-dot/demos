Retail banks identify creditworthy audiences for product campaigns before sending a single offer — without any party accessing raw consumer credit data. The clean room validates eligibility and returns only FCRA-compliant pre-screened segments.

Use Case 03
Look-Alike Modeling
Use your pre-screen qualifying population as a high-quality seed audience. The clean room generates aggregate feature profiles of qualifying individuals. Your team builds LAL models to find creditworthy net-new prospects beyond the direct match pool.
Viewing as:
The three parties
Credit Bureau
Credit attribute profiles
Retail Bank
Seed population & model team
Data Provider
Universe for scoring & activation
How it works
1
Qualifying segment becomes the seed
Pre-screen qualifying population from Use Case 01 is passed as the seed input. No individual records — only the token set of qualifiers.
Retail Bank
2
Clean room generates aggregate feature profile
Behavioral and credit signals of the seed population — income index, revolving behavior, tenure — returned as a privacy-safe aggregate profile.
DCR Engine
3
Bank builds LAL model against the profile
Data science team uses the aggregate feature profile to score the full provider universe. Net-new creditworthy prospects are identified.
Bank Data Science
4
LAL audience activated to digital channels
Match-keyed LAL audience pushed to programmatic DSPs, CTV, and paid social for upper-funnel reach with credit-quality guardrails.
Data Provider
What the bank receives
Extended creditworthy reach beyond the direct match pool
Aggregate feature profile of qualifying seed
LAL model scoring of provider universe
Match-keyed audience for DSP / CTV activation
Incremental reach metrics vs. direct match
LAL modeling uses only aggregate profiles. No individual credit attributes cross party boundaries.
